Learn About Leaf Diseases

Leaf diseases can significantly reduce crop yield and quality. Early identification allows farmers to apply proper treatments, prevent disease spread, and improve overall plant health. Leaf Scan helps by using Artificial Intelligence to recognize diseases from leaf images within seconds.

Capture Clear Images

Use good lighting and focus on the leaf. Avoid blurry images or cluttered backgrounds for better prediction accuracy.

Healthy Plants

Healthy plants have vibrant color, strong stems, and leaves without unusual spots, mold, yellowing, or curling.

Disease Symptoms

Common symptoms include brown or black spots, yellow patches, mold growth, leaf curling, wilting, and premature leaf drop.

Prevention Tips

  • Inspect plants regularly.
  • Avoid excessive watering.
  • Ensure proper sunlight and air circulation.
  • Remove infected leaves immediately.
  • Use disease-resistant crop varieties.
  • Apply fertilizers and pesticides responsibly.
  • Practice crop rotation.
  • Keep farming tools clean and disinfected.

How to Use Leaf Scan

  1. Upload a leaf image or open the live camera.
  2. Capture a clear photo of the leaf.
  3. Click Predict.
  4. View the predicted disease and confidence score.
  5. Use the result to decide the next steps for treatment or expert consultation.
