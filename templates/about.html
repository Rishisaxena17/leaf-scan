About Leaf Scan

Leaf Scan is an Artificial Intelligence powered web application that helps identify diseases in plant leaves from a single photograph. Using deep learning and computer vision, our system analyzes leaf images and predicts the most likely disease within seconds.


The goal of Leaf Scan is to support farmers, gardeners, researchers, and students by providing a fast and accessible way to detect plant diseases. Early diagnosis enables timely treatment, reduces crop losses, and promotes healthier agricultural practices.

Our Mission

To make AI-based plant disease detection simple, accurate, and accessible to everyone, improving crop health and agricultural productivity.

What We Do

Our deep learning model analyzes uploaded or captured leaf images and predicts diseases across multiple crops, including tomato, potato, rice, corn, grape, apple, cassava, pepper, peach, strawberry, squash, orange, and cherry.

Technology

Leaf Scan is built using TensorFlow, EfficientNet, Flask, OpenCV, HTML, CSS, and JavaScript to deliver fast, reliable, and user-friendly predictions.
